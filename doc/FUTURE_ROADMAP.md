# 1. Purpose

This document defines the long-term technical roadmap of the Fateh Music Academy platform.

The roadmap establishes a strategic vision for future development while ensuring that architectural consistency is preserved.

The platform is designed to evolve without requiring fundamental redesign.

---

# 2. Vision

The long-term objective is to build

> The most complete Persian music education platform with world-class architecture, semantic SEO and AI-native capabilities.

The roadmap emphasizes sustainable evolution rather than rapid expansion.
